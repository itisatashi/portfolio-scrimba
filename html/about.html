My Coding Journey

Hey there! I’m Atashi, a passionate learner and aspiring front-end developer. Welcome to Journey Through Code, my personal learning journal where I document my growth, challenges, and discoveries in the world of web development.

My Learning Journey

As a digital economy student with a deep interest in technology, I’ve always been fascinated by how the web works. To turn my passion into a career, I started learning front-end development online. What began as casual learning soon turned into a structured journey—building projects, experimenting with code, and refining my skills every day.

To stay consistent, I decided to share my progress. Writing about what I learn helps me understand concepts better, and at the same time, it might inspire others who are on a similar path.

Why I Created This Journal

At first, I was just taking notes for myself. But I realized that documenting my experiences and lessons online could be valuable—not just for me, but for anyone learning to code. This journal is a space where I reflect on my progress, share insights, and stay motivated as I work toward becoming a front-end developer.

If you’re on a learning journey too, I hope my experiences can encourage and support you. Let’s grow together!
