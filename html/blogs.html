JULY 23, 2022

My new journey as a Scrimba student.

After several months of learning in the Frontend Developer Career Path, I'm continuing to improve my skills through Scrimba’s interactive lessons. Every day, I challenge myself with new projects, refining my coding abilities and building a strong foundation for my future career as a front-end developer.

How I stay committed to learning

I like to think of myself as a lifelong learner, always exploring new ideas. As a digital economy student with a strong passion for technology, I spend hours learning front-end development, experimenting with code, and trying to apply new techniques to my projects.

While that has been exciting, I realized that sharing my journey could help not only me but also others who are on the same path. That’s why I started documenting my learning process—so I could track my progress and, at the same time, contribute to the developer community.

How I got started

I started with small steps and gradually built my Journey Through Code site as my personal learning journal. I took notes on everything I learned, whether it was a new JavaScript concept or a CSS trick. After each learning session, I would reflect on the key points and rewrite them in my own words.

This process helped me truly understand what I was learning instead of just memorizing it. Over time, I saw that sharing my learning summaries wasn’t just helping me—it was also inspiring others who were learning to code. That motivation keeps me going, pushing me to keep learning, improving, and sharing my journey.
